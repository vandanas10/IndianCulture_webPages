Yoga

Yoga originated in ancient India over 5,000 years ago and aims to unite the body, mind, and spirit through physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation. It is widely practiced for its health and wellness benefits.

Greeting

Indian greetings vary based on region, religion, and occasion. Some common ones include Namaste, Salaam, and Sat Sri Akal. Respect and warmth are emphasized.

Festival

India is known for its diverse festivals, celebrated with joy and enthusiasm. Diwali, Holi, and Dussehra are some popular festivals that bring people together.

Dance

Indian dance fuses classical and folk styles. Classical forms, like Kathak and Bharatanatyam, emphasize grace. Folk dances showcase regional customs and music.

Martial Arts

India has a rich history of martial arts, including Kalaripayattu, Silambam, Gatka, and Thang-Ta. These arts combine physical skill with mental discipline and spiritual development.
